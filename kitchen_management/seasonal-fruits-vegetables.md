# Seasonal Fruits And Vegetables
This page captures seasonal fruits and vegetables in month wise order


**January**
- Upto 10th/15th
  - `jujube or regi pandu or Indian plum` in street vendors
  - `raw channa dal` in super markets and street vendors
  - `raddish` is available in super markets
  - `sweet potato` is available in supermarkets and street vendors
- After 10th/15th
   - `raddish` is available in super markets
   - `green grapes` season start. do not buy them at this period. they may be too sour
   -  `sweet potato` is available in supermarkets and street vendors
