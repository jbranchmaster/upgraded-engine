# Green Chilli Karam For Dosa
This karam is made from green chillies and onions. This is used for dosa.


# Core Ingredients
- `30-32` light green chillies
- `125 grams` onions. Cut each onion into 8 pieces.
- `cooking oil` (we use sunflower oil)
- `salt` to taste

# Preparation
- Take 1 spoon `cooking oil` into a small vessel and heat it a bit
- Add the `light green chillies` into this vessel and let the chillies cook
- The `light green chillies` should become little white in colour and soft
- Turn off the stove and let it cool
- Add the `cut onions` , `light green chilles` and `salt` to mixer
- Blend it all together in whip mode. The texture should be little rough and not smooth


# Serving
This is used for egg dosa. Its applied on top of the egg on dosa.
