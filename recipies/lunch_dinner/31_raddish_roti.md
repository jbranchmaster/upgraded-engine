# Raddish Roti

Raddish roti is made from raddish and wheat. This is usually available in winters.


# Core Ingredients

- `1` Raddish. Wash, peel the skin and greate it. Take grated raddish into a muslin cloth and squeeze it to remove all the water. 
- `2-3` green chilles finely chopped
- `1 teaspoon` saunf
- `1 teaspoon` vaum (ajwain seeds/carom seeds)
- salt as needed
- turmeric as needed
- `1` sprig corriander leaves
- cooking oil. We used `sunflower` cooking oil


# Preparation
- Take a vessel and add `1 spoon cooking oil` into it and heat it.
- Take `vaum` into it and let it pop. Do not over cook.
- Next add `saunf` and let it cook a bit.
- Next add chopped `green chilli` and let it cook a bit.
- Next add `salt` and `turmeric` and let it cook a bit.
- Next add grated `raddish` and cook until it becomes slight brown. This step is with closed lid.
- Next add `corriander leaves` and cook for 2 mins.
- Once cooled the cooked raddish masala is mixed with `roti batter` to make rotis.
